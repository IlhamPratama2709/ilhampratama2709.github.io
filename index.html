01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a final-year Economics undergraduate at Universitas Indonesia, passionate about understanding how economic forces shape cities, regions, and the environment. My academic journey has been driven by a deep curiosity for empirical research and a desire to produce work that matters.

Over the past few years, I've had the privilege of interning at Bank Indonesia — contributing to CGE modeling and foreign exchange reserve reporting — as well as working with Lembaga Demografi FEB UI and the ARC Institute on longitudinal data harmonization.

Beyond the classroom, I've competed and won at national and ASEAN-level research competitions, spanning topics from infrastructure economics to renewable energy policy. I believe good research should be rigorous, relevant, and readable.
